Green Sauce Pasta

Add cooked spinach, garlic, Parmesan cheese, cream cheese and broth to a food processor and puree until a sauce is formed. Combine: Add sauce back into pan along with cooked pasta. Add pinch of salt and pepper to taste. Cook another 2-3 minutes to heat through.
